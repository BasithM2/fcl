FIDA CHAMPIONS LEAQUE

The FCL (Fida Champions League) is an exciting football tournament featuring four teams: Kylaq, Kasaab, Ultraas, and Cosmos. Matches are held at the NPS Ground in Kalamassery, Ernakulam, culminating in a thrilling final at the Kaloor Turf. The league consists of six matches leading up to the championship, showcasing intense competition and skill among the teams. Fans can expect an electrifying atmosphere as the teams battle for glory!
